okta-automation Co. Ltd

Located in Guangdong Province, China, okta-automation Co. Ltd is a key manufacturing base in the Pearl River Delta region. The company occupies over 15,000 square meters and adheres to a customer-centric and people-oriented business philosophy. It is committed to enhancing product competitiveness through high-quality and innovative products, and aims to build mutually beneficial cooperation with customers.
